Top 5 Coolest Art in Boardgames

By William Wall



5: Root

Root is really fun game with cartoonish art.

The goal of the game is to control your forest and build your up kingdom



4: Brass

Brass is really cool looking game but it too dark to see most of the details.

Brass is a ok game, how it works is by getting ports and then trading with players to bulid more ports.



3: Gloom Haven

This game is the best RPG board game out there, the role playing and the story telling is just amazing, it's too bad most of the art is on the box then in the game or I would of put this higher on the list.

This game is about 1 to 4 players fighting togather for money, food, and their freedom.



2: Wingspan

Wingspan is a great game with really nice art. Most of the art is illustrations of birds, and the boards look just as good as well.

The game is about buying bird cards to get food, eggs, and more bird cards. Each bird card has a cost to play, and to pay the cost you need to gather food and egg to do so...



1: Scythe

This game is one of my favorite games, with the story, the strategy, and the art beyond anything else. The game just work so well with everything together.

This game about using worker to get resoures and using the resoures to get money. The game ends when the 6 stars is placed, each star is gained by finshing a goal, at the end of the game it not about who place the last star that wins but who has the most money.
